ABOUT

Marília is a Japanese-Brazilian-Canadian artist living in Vancouver. She is a third-year student at UBC studying Theatre Design and Production. As a 'Jack of all trades', she has worked in scenic painting and costume construction but specializes in prop-making. She is always eager to learn a new craft and dabbles in a variety of roles in her program.
